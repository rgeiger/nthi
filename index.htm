An INVITATION from Tom McGovern and Allan Ross

Night to Honor Israel Executive Director and Jewish Federation of the Quad Cities Executive Director

We are pleased to invite you to attend the Quad Cities 9th annual Night to Honor Israel, October 18th, at Calvary Church of the Quad Cities, 7pm. The Lord has bestowed a blessing on our community since 2003 just as is promised in Genesis 12:3 "I will bless those who bless you and curse those who curse you. Guest Speaker: Pastor Michael Stevens

CUFI AFRICAN AMERICAN OUTREACH DIRECTOR

Night to Honor Israel Executive Director and Alan Ross, Jewish Federation of the Quad Cities Executive Director Pastor Stevens, founded the University City Church of God in Christ in Charlotte, NC. Beginning with only a handful of members, this ministry has surpassed 1,700 members in just a short time. He is a graduate of North Carolina A&T State University. Additionally, he has received a Masters of Practical Theology from Oral Roberts University and is currently pursuing his doctorate at ORU. Pastor Stevens serves on the Executive Board of the National Church Growth Conference of the Church of God in Christ.
